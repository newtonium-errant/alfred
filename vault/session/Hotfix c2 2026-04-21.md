- **Scope-correct refusal can still be wrong.** Salem's prior behavior ("that's outside my scope, try Claude Code in an IDE") was technically accurate — but it missed the obvious next step (KAL-LE exists). Scope rules need to be paired with awareness of alternatives, or they'll dead-end the user. Pattern to watch for elsewhere: any SKILL section that says "you can't do X" without saying "but Y can."
